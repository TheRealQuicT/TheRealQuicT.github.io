Crafting Dreams Into Reality

At Haven Builds, we specialize in a wide range of construction services designed to enhance and elevate your home. As owner operators, we construct in-house which means we are properly able to manage quality and provide more value for your money. Working with us involves seeing familiar faces, having a reliable point of contact on site, cleanliness and honesty. With our extensive knowledge of carpentry and building science, we understand the details which ensures our work is built to last. Whether it be a small deck or a full custom build home, our excellent service and enthusiasm remains the same.

Additions

We take pride in building high-quality home additions that enhance your living space and lifestyle. We focus on providing a seamless integration with your existing home, ensuring your addition feels like a natural extension of your space. Let us help you create the perfect solution to meet your needs and bring your vision to life.

Renovations

Making your home feel brand new by blending modern design with energy-efficient upgrades. Our renovations breathe new life into your space while reducing energy costs. We focus on creating a comfortable, sustainable environment that combines the charm of your home with the latest in modern amenities and eco-friendly solutions. Whether it’s a basement in-law suite, new kitchen, bathroom or deck, we’d be happy to schedule a free consultation to discuss your design. Let us help you transform your home into a stylish, energy-efficient Haven that suits your needs for years to come.

Laneway & Garden Suites

We believe laneway/garden suites are a fantastic solution to Toronto’s housing challenges, offering both additional living space for a growing family or additional income by rental. Our team specializes in the challenges that come with constructing these suites in Toronto and come up with a process to deliver your suite within 4 months! Contact us now for a free consultation and we’d be happy to determine the eligibility of your lot.

Framing

Whether it’s a custom home, addition, laneway/garden or deck framing, we approach each job with precision and attention to detail. We pride ourselves on delivering framing that provides a solid foundation for the rest of the construction process. With years of experience and a commitment to quality, our team ensures your project is completed on time, on budget, and built to last. Our emphasis on building science ensures our builds to perform better than most structures built today.

Meet The Team

Get to know the people behind Haven Builds.

Sidney Phillips

Owner

Sidney's journey began in Fredericton, NB, where his love for construction and craftsmanship was nurtured from a young age. Sidney is not just a carpenter; he's a master of his craft. With over a decade of hands-on experience in custom home building. His expertise and meticulous attention to detail set the standard for excellence at Haven Builds. Sidney's passion doesn't stop at building; he loves sharing his knowledge and skills with others. He finds joy in teaching construction techniques and empowering the next generation of builders. His hands-on approach and enthusiasm make learning an inspiring and engaging experience.

Thanoj Thangarajah

Owner

Born and raised in Mississauga, ON, Thanoj’s love for construction was ignited during his childhood and has only grown stronger over the years. Thanoj studied environmental management at the University of New Brunswick, where he developed a deep passion for sustainable and eco-friendly building practices. With over five years of experience in the field, Thanoj excels in project management, ensuring every project is completed efficiently and sustainably. His expertise ensures that our builds are not only beautiful but also environmentally responsible.
